What is a Spotter?

Spotter:
Someone who aids our cause by volunteering for live text updates for missing people in their area.

According to Statistics Canada, 800 000 children are reported missing each year, roughly 2,000 per day. By signing up for text updates, you are helping us widen our range and search for these people faster. Someone's sibling, parent, best-friend and child can be found much faster by increasing awareness of who is missing. Simply sign up below to stay updated in your area.
